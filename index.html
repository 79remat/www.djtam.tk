About Me






Click to Listen to My LIVE Mixes

Please allow me to introduce myself... My Name is Tamer and I am the owner of Erie DJ Company and headlining DJ. I have been DJing weddings, parties, and events in the Western PA area for over 6 years, working with various DJ Companies.

Growing up I studied music and played multiple instruments including guitar, piano, and violin. I pride myself on having great knowledge of all music from the classics to today's hits.

After recently finishing my degree in Computer Science, I knew that I wanted to follow my passion and continue my career as a DJ. I am a true professional who is very personable and will provide you with amazing service!

The Process

1. Book

Scheule a call to get a free price quote! After that, all you need to do is sign our service agreement and pay your deposit to lock in DJ services for your date!

2. Plan

Fill out our in-depth Reception Planning Guide that we use with our clients so you can see how we will work with you to make sure all the details of your reception are planned out perfectly.

3. Relax

My goal is to make this process stress-free and have entertainment be the last of your worries on your big day!
