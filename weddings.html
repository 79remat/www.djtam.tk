Why Choose Me?




Weddings Are My Specialty!

Collectively I have over 7 years of experience rocking weddings & events. A few of my peers & I started our own group of wedding DJ companies because of our love for the business. Over the years, I have had the opportunity to be a part several companies awarded multiple "Best of Wedding Awards" and "Weddings Hall of Fame" by The Knot.

We've also won Best of awards from Pittsburgh Magazine, Thumbtack and Yelp and were named one of the top 15 DJ companies in Pittsburgh by www.expertise.com. I am also very proud that I have been able to maintain a 5 star review status since I started DJing.
